Tips & Advice
5 Simple Grounding Techniques That Actually Work

When anxiety hits, these grounding techniques have been lifesavers for me:

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Technique: Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Cold Water: Splash cold water on your face or hold an ice cube
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and release each muscle group
  • Box Breathing: 4 counts in, hold 4, out 4, hold 4
  • Mindful Walking: Focus on each step and how your feet feel
