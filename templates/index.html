Mann Ki Baat 2.0 – Because Doston Ko Bhi Kabhi Sunna Chahiye!

Safe, confidential, and fun space for students to share thoughts, beat stress, and find balance in life.

How it Works ?


1. Sign Up (or Stay Anonymous 🤫) : Just share a few basic details (or none at all). We respect your privacy – no roll numbers here!

2. Pick What’s on Your Mind 🎯 : Select from 8 life areas – career, relationships, health, etc. This helps us understand you better.

3. Share Your Mann Ki Baat 🗣️ : Write it out, say it out loud, or even vent in Hinglish. Our AI buddy listens without judgment.

4. Get Instant Support 🤖✨ : Get empathetic responses, personalized suggestions, and daily affirmations to feel lighter.

5. Grow With It 🌱 : Track your mood, perform fun activities, and slowly build a healthier, calmer, and happier YOU.


Why “Mann Ki Baat 2.0”? It’ll blow your mind.

🎓 Made for Students – From exam pressure and endless assignments to peer drama and career FOMO – we know student life isn’t easy. This space is designed with your struggles in mind.

🔒 Confidential & Non-judgy – Here, you can vent without worrying about “log kya kahenge.” Your identity is safe, your thoughts are private, and your words won’t be shared with anyone.

✨ Features That Help You Out – With six AI-powered features, Mann Ki Baat 2.0 is here to listen, guide, and support you every step of the way.

📱 Easy, Quick & Always There – No complicated setups. Just log in, choose what’s bugging you, and start sharing. Anytime & Anywhere, Mann Ki Baat 2.0 is ready to listen.

What You’ll Get Inside?


💬 AI Chatbot – Your 24/7 digital dost. Share stress, rants, or random thoughts in English, Hindi, or Hinglish. It listens without judgment and responds with empathy, or friendly gyaan.

🚨 Crisis Detection – When things get overwhelming, our AI can sense it. It gently alerts you, offers calming support, and guides you towards safe, confidential helplines if needed.

👥 Community – An anonymous student space where you can share your struggles, read others’ stories, and find comfort in knowing you’re not alone – all in a safe environment.

🎮 Activities & Challenges – Self-care can be fun! Try journaling prompts, mindfulness streaks, and daily challenges while earning badges and tracking progress.

✨ Daily Affirmations –Start or end your day on a positive note. Get motivating texts, soothing voice notes, or even short videos that remind you of your strength and potential.

🛌 Lifestyle Tips – Student life can be chaotic – late nights, junk food, zero balance. Our AI suggests small, practical fixes for better sleep, diet, and focus, tailored to your routine.

Aim of Mann Ki Baat 2.0

To transform student stress into calmness by providing a safe, confidential, and empathetic space where every student can share their thoughts freely, find support, and walk away lighter, happier, and more confident.
